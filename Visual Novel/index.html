Damage: 0
Bones: 0

RULES:

1) Draw either from the squirrel or your deck.

2) A card is playable if it has no cost.

3) If it costs blood, you will need to sacrifice a card from the board.

4) If it costs bones, you will have to consume the given number of bones. Bones are collected when a friendly card dies.

5) Click the attack button to attack. After, click on the enemy turn button to let your enemy play.

6) When there is no enemy card, the card attacks directly and inflicts damage.

7) When there is an enemy card, the card will attack and deal damage to it.

8) The first one to reach 10 damage wins.
